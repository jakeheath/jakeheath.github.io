The Wonders of Space

Space is the biggest wonder of our species. It can easily be described as:

  • Beautiful
  • Monstorous
  • Scary
  • Immense

Of all the things it can be described as, the most accurate of all is simply that it is beyond elegant. It stretches infinitely for all we know, and we don't know. It is filled with uncountable galaxies, stars, planets. Probably thousands of species of intelligent life spread through our universe.
